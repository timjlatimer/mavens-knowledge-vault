## Section 3 — Posting Strategy

**Phase 1 — Seed (Week 1):** Tim posts Draft A in Mamas for Mamas and Red Deer Free Food and Essentials. These are the warmest, most community-oriented groups. The toilet paper hook is tangible and shareable. Watch for engagement and DMs.

**Phase 2 — Expand (Week 2):** Post Draft B in Red Deer Rentals and Frugal Living in Alberta. The rent/financial angle resonates with a broader audience. These groups have province-wide reach.

**Phase 3 — Deepen (Week 3):** Post Draft C in Red Deer Community and Red Deer Mommy Chat. The "real talk" tone builds trust with moms who have seen too many ads and not enough help.

**Ongoing:** Monitor comments. Respond personally (not as a brand). If someone asks "what's the catch?" — answer honestly: "There isn't one. Grace is free. The TP is real. We're trying to change how things work for people like us." Let the community do the marketing after the first few genuine interactions.
