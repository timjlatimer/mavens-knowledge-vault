### 2.2 Need Milk Money (NMM)

**Source:** `mavens-knowledge-vault/knowledge-base/MAVEN_PROJECT.md`, `mavens-knowledge-vault/briefs/polsia-strategic-scoring-hb1000-initiatives.md`

A micro-lending program providing $25, $50, or $100 allotments with a flat $2.50 administration fee, no interest, no penalties, and no collections. NMM replaced the V1 "Community Chest" model. It is controlled by Grace and Big Mama. If Ruby Red cannot repay, the step-down logic absorbs it gracefully. **Why It Matters for Ruby Red:** A $25 shortfall before payday can cascade into a $35 NSF fee, a $50 late fee, and a month of financial stress. NMM breaks the cascade at the source for $2.50.

**Build Complexity:** High (requires regulatory review and licensed financial services partner)
