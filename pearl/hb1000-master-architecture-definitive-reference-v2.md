- **Gamification Engine Integration:** The Ticker Tape is now the primary output for the Gamification Engine, displaying personalized, non-shaming gamification updates (e.g., "You've saved $5 this week! That's a 3-day streak!").

### LAYER 8: THE ORGANIZATIONAL LAYER (LDP)
- **AI Fairness & Bias Mitigation as Core Component:** The LDP now includes a formal **AI Fairness & Bias Mitigation Framework**. The "Council of Rivals" (adversarial LLM debate) is the primary technical tool for bias detection and auditing.

### LAYER 9: THE CERTIFICATION & TRUST LAYER
- **Data Cooperative as Prerequisite for Blue Seal:** The Blue Seal certification now **requires** that the certified entity operate on a **Data Cooperative model**. This makes data sovereignty a non-negotiable component of integrity.

### LAYER 10: THE FUNDING ENGINE
- **Ruby Red Emergency Fund as New Component:** This layer now includes the **Ruby Red Emergency Fund** as a major new component, sitting alongside the 10% Tithe and the 50/50 Moonshot fund. The "Need Milk Money" program is the foundational infrastructure for deploying these funds.

### LAYER 11: THE GOVERNANCE ENGINE
- **AI Fairness & Bias Mitigation Governance:** The Learning Loop is now the official governance mechanism for the AI Fairness & Bias Mitigation Framework. The "Three Guardians" (Devil's Advocate, North Star's Voice, Pragmatist) are responsible for reviewing bias audit reports and recommending action.

### LAYER 12: THE MAVEN SERVICE REGISTRY
- **Maven as a Data Cooperative:** Maven is now explicitly defined as a **Data Cooperative**. This is its foundational legal and technical structure, not a feature.

### THE GUARDIAN LAYER: PEARL
- **Voice-First AI Orchestration:** Grace's voice is now the primary interface for the **Dialect-Specific Voice AI**. Pearl is responsible for orchestrating the training, deployment, and continuous improvement of these voice models.
