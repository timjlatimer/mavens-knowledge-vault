# Maven Soul Statement

> **This is not a feature spec. This is the soul of the entire platform.**

---

Maven stands in there when life gets real. Regardless of the circumstances. That is not charity — that is social impact capitalism. Purpose with profit, but purpose first.

We are not an NGO. We are not a charity. We are a business that believes moving people toward the best version of themselves IS the business model.

Ruby Red is not a customer to be managed — she is a human being to be believed in.

We do not leave when it gets hard. We stand in there.

---

*Governing philosophy of the Maven platform. Authored by Tim Latimer, Chief Visionary Officer, SIC HB1000 Solve Team. Committed to the vault as the North Star against which every feature, every agent, every line of code, and every business decision is measured.*

*If a feature does not serve this statement, it does not ship.*

*If a business decision contradicts this statement, it does not stand.*

*If an agent's behavior violates this statement, it is broken and must be fixed.*
