# PEARL Operating Model — Communication & Assessment Notes
## From User Input (Feb 14, 2026)

## Key Principles Extracted

### 1. Bingo Card Champion ↔ Coach Relationship
- It's a LOOPING process, iteration process, learning process between bingo card champion and coach
- Champions bring BAD NEWS UP (one-way channel) — never poison the well by going sideways
- Bad news goes to the higher-up HB1000 to be dealt with
- "Autopsy without blame" iteration — looking for improvements
- Improvements must be transparent: brought UP the line, ACROSS channels, and DOWN into channels
- 360-degree robust communication in a radical sense

### 2. The "Poison the Well" Exception
- The ONLY thing NOT shared in 360-degree communication: stuff about other people, HR-type issues
- If you have fears/elephant-in-the-room issues → bring to your DIRECT REPORT in the org chart
- Working sideways (sharing fears with peers) = poisoning the well
- Direct report helps you work through it

### 3. Daily Cadence — The Daily Stand-Up
- Every day. Not stored for a long time.
- Daily briefing time every morning — set in calendar (e.g., 7:00 AM)
- Part of the "daily stand-up meeting"
- You learn everything going on in YOUR bingo card AND the other bingo cards around you
- This becomes regular practice

### 4. Pearl's Monthly Assessment (Pearl Number 3)
- Pearl does ongoing checking (daily)
- Once a month: formal REPORT OUT on each Bingo Card
- Report goes to: (a) the person managing the Bingo Card, AND (b) their direct report
- Pearl advises on how the person in charge has done with their initiatives
- Checks: met budgets? KPIs? Tracking toward goals?

### 5. KPI Assessment Process
- Assessment of the person's ABILITY to fill their roles inside the Bingo Card initiative
- End of month: source of truth comes in (proper data, CFO numbers for that initiative)
- Finance numbers needed to generate KPIs
- KPIs are generated, verified
- Pearl assesses: is the person's effort good enough, or do they need improvement?

### 6. The B-Chart Assessment
- Honest, transparent assessment of role fit
- Questions: Is this the right role for you? Are you in a sweet spot? Are you doing your best?
- If improvement needed: what tools and training would get them there?
- B-Chart is a specific assessment methodology (user offered to share details)

### 7. The Mode Switch Concept
- After the HB1000 center point, there's a SWITCH
- Personal mode: just your personal life stuff (no switch needed, goes directly)
- Initiative mode: switch into your bingo card initiatives
- Some people only have personal mode
- Others can switch between personal and initiative/business mode

### 8. The Big Picture
- This is a MANUAL for any HB1000 on how to live life once you get to maturity
- How to line yourself up with: spiritual north star → yourself → influence on the world → circle of influence
- "You grow people and they grow themselves, or they grow the business"
- This is a TRAINING MANUAL on how to grow people
