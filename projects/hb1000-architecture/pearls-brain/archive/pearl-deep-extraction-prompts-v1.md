# Pearl Deep Extraction Prompts
## One-Time Full Knowledge Harvest — Every Platform, Every Conversation

**Version:** 1.0 — February 14, 2026
**Author:** SIC HB1000 Solve Team
**Purpose:** One-time deep extraction of ALL conversation history from every AI platform into Pearl's Knowledge Vault. This is the "initial population" — the Courier agents handle ongoing harvesting after this.

---

## How This Works

These are NOT the ongoing Courier prompts. These are **one-time deep extraction prompts** designed to dump EVERYTHING each platform remembers about your conversations. You paste one into each platform, it runs, and you deliver the output to Pearl.

**The process:**
1. Open each platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok)
2. Start a NEW conversation
3. Paste the appropriate extraction prompt below
4. Let it run — it will produce a massive structured output
5. Copy the output and either:
   - Email it to yourself with subject: `PEARL VAULT INITIAL: [Platform Name]`
   - Paste it into Pearl's Knowledge Vault intake in the dashboard
6. Repeat for each platform

5. IMPORTANT: Do NOT summarize or editorialize. Give me the RAW knowledge. Pearl will synthesize it.
