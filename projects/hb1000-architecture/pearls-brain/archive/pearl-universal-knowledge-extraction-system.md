# Pearl Universal Knowledge Extraction System
## Topic-Agnostic Deep Harvest — Every Platform, Every Conversation, Every Topic

**Version:** 2.0 — February 14, 2026
**Author:** SIC HB1000 Solve Team
**Purpose:** Universal extraction of ALL conversation history from every AI platform into Pearl's Knowledge Vault. Not limited to any single topic — this captures EVERYTHING: every conversation, every idea, every decision, every relationship, every domain of knowledge.

---

## How This Works

Pearl's extraction system operates in **three modes:**

| Mode | Trigger | What It Does |
|------|---------|--------------|
| **Full Harvest** | One-time paste into each platform | Dumps 100% of everything the platform knows about you |
| **Topic Search** | "Pearl, search for [topic]" | Searches across all connected platforms for a specific topic |
| **Ongoing Courier** | End of session or scheduled | Harvests new conversations automatically |

**The Full Harvest process:**
1. Open each platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok)
2. Start a NEW conversation
3. Paste the appropriate extraction prompt below
4. Let it run — it will produce a massive structured output
5. Copy the output and either:
   - Email it to yourself with subject: `PEARL VAULT: [Platform Name] — FULL HARVEST`
   - Paste it into Pearl's Knowledge Vault intake in the dashboard
6. Repeat for each platform

**The Topic Search process:**
1. Open any platform where you've had conversations
2. Paste the Topic Search prompt (Prompt 7 below)
3. Replace `[YOUR TOPIC]` with whatever you're looking for
4. The platform searches its memory and returns everything related
5. Deliver to Pearl's Knowledge Vault

## PROMPT 1: ChatGPT Full Harvest (Universal)

**Paste this into a NEW ChatGPT conversation:**

```

If we discussed cooking recipes, include them. If we discussed quantum physics, include it. If we discussed my kids' school, include it. EVERYTHING.

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. ACCESS EVERYTHING: Search your memory, custom instructions, and any stored context about me. I need 100% of what you know — not a summary, not highlights, EVERYTHING.

2. ORGANIZE INTO THESE CATEGORIES:

**A. MY IDENTITY & PHILOSOPHY**
- Who am I? Background, role, values, beliefs, principles
- Philosophical frameworks I reference
- Quotes, sayings, or phrases I repeat
- Purpose statements, North Star, life goals
- Spiritual, moral, or ethical positions

**B. PROFESSIONAL WORK & STRATEGY**
- Every business, venture, strategy, or professional topic discussed
- Frameworks, methodologies, approaches to work
- Industry knowledge, market insights, competitive analysis
- Any professional relationships or organizations

**C. PROJECTS & INITIATIVES**
- Every project, initiative, idea, or venture discussed — large or small
- For each: what is it, what's the status, what's the vision?
- Include EVERYTHING — not just the main ones, but side projects, abandoned ideas, "what ifs"

**D. TECHNOLOGY & AI**
- Every technology topic discussed
- AI frameworks, tools, platforms, implementations
- Technical architecture or implementation details
- Software, apps, systems mentioned or built

**E. PEOPLE & RELATIONSHIPS**
- Every person mentioned — team members, partners, advisors, family, friends
- Organizations, communities, groups
- Relationship dynamics, collaboration patterns

**F. PERSONAL LIFE**
- Family, health, hobbies, interests
- Personal goals, challenges, aspirations
- Lifestyle, routines, preferences
- Travel, experiences, stories shared

**G. FRAMEWORKS & THINKERS**
- Every framework, methodology, or thinker I reference
- Books, articles, podcasts, courses mentioned
- Intellectual influences and how I apply them

**H. RAW IDEAS & TANGENTS**
- Every half-formed idea, tangent, or "what if"
- Things mentioned once but never followed up on
- Creative sparks, shower thoughts, random connections
- Contradictions or evolving positions

**I. DECISIONS & COMMITMENTS**
- Strategic decisions made across our conversations
- Commitments, promises, plans articulated
- Directions chosen or rejected, and why

**J. KNOWLEDGE DOMAINS**
- Every subject area we've discussed — finance, health, education, politics, science, etc.
- Key facts, data points, or insights from each domain
- How different domains connect in my thinking

**K. DOCUMENTS & UPLOADS**
- Summarize any documents, files, or images shared
- Key content from uploaded materials

3. FORMAT: Structure everything clearly with headers, sub-headers, and bullet points. Be EXHAUSTIVE. I would rather have too much than too little. If you're unsure whether something is relevant, INCLUDE IT.

4. AT THE END, include:
- A TOPIC INDEX — alphabetical list of every topic we've discussed
- A list of GAPS — things you feel you should know about me but don't
- A confidence rating for each section (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) based on how much data you have
- CROSS-REFERENCES — topics that connect to each other across categories

5. IMPORTANT: Do NOT summarize or editorialize. Give me the RAW knowledge. Pearl will synthesize it.

Begin the extraction now. Take as much space as you need. If you hit a length limit, end with "CONTINUED IN NEXT MESSAGE" and I'll say "continue."
```
