# Bingo Hotel & HB1000 Model - Extraction Notes

**Project:** HB1000 Ecosystem Architecture
**North Star:** To build a comprehensive, multi-layered model for the SIC HB1000 ecosystem.
**Document Type:** Raw Notes / Extraction
**Date:** 2026-03-16

---

## 1. Hard Hat Tour

*   Concept of a "Hard Hat Tour" as a metaphor for exploring and understanding the foundational layers of the HB1000 ecosystem.
*   Implies a focus on the underlying infrastructure, the "plumbing" and "wiring" of the system.

## 2. Rooftop Society

*   The "Rooftop Society" represents the highest level of the HB1000 ecosystem.
*   This is the realm of strategic decision-making, vision, and long-term planning.
*   Membership is exclusive to key stakeholders (Tim HB1000, Master Jeeves, etc.).

## 3. Three-Layer HB1000 Model

*   A conceptual model of the HB1000 ecosystem with three distinct layers:
    1.  **The Foundation (Hard Hat Tour):** The core infrastructure, data, and protocols.
    2.  **The Experience Layer:** The user-facing applications, interfaces, and services.
    3.  **The Strategic Layer (Rooftop Society):** The high-level governance, vision, and decision-making.
