## Slide 2: The Core Problem: Goal Drift
- **Headline**: Iterative improvement without alignment checks leads to "Goal Drift"
- **Key Points**:
  - The "Telephone Game Effect": V2 improves on V1, V3 improves on V2... but by V10, the output may be disconnected from the original request.
  - External innovation (Phase 2) can *increase* this risk by injecting exciting but distracting ideas.
  - Result: Outputs that are "better" by some metrics but fail to solve the user's actual problem.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution: A Three-Phase, Aligned Protocol
- **Headline**: Combining Correctness, Competitiveness, and Integrity
- **Key Points**:
  - **Phase 1 (Engine A)**: System of Record — ensures the output is *correct*.
  - **Phase 2 (Engine B)**: System of Innovation — ensures the output is *competitive*.
  - **Phase 3 (Guardian)**: System of Alignment — ensures the output has *integrity*.

---

## Slide 4: Phase 1 — System of Record (Correctness)
- **Headline**: The Baseline Quality Gate ensures outputs meet a minimum standard of 80/100
- **Key Points**:
  - **The Four-Question Process**: SCORE → GAP → FIX → ACTION
  - **Five Dimensions** (20 pts each): Completeness, Clarity, Accuracy, Depth, Actionability
  - If score < 80, the AI MUST fix and re-assess before proceeding.

---

## Slide 5: Phase 2 — System of Innovation (Competitiveness)
- **Headline**: The "Best Practice Junkie" scan drives outputs from "good enough" to "state-of-the-art"
- **Key Points**:
  - Begins only AFTER Phase 1 is passed (score ≥ 80).
  - **Mandatory Innovation Scan**: Identify gaps, research externally, find Gold Standards.
  - **Four Dimensions** (25 pts each): Clarity, Completeness, Impact, Elegance.
  - Uses a Continue/Stop decision framework based on diminishing returns.

---

## Slide 6: Phase 3 — System of Alignment (Integrity) — THE NEW GUARDIAN
- **Headline**: The Guardian prevents Goal Drift by checking the "Spirit" of the original request
- **Key Points**:
  - A separate "Editor-in-Chief" persona, blind to Phases 1 & 2.
  - Compares the final output against the **North Star** (the original user request).
  - A single "Fail" sends the deliverable back to Phase 1 for rework.

---

## Slide 7: The Three-Question "Spirit Check"
- **Headline**: Three questions the Guardian must answer before final delivery
- **Content**:
  1. **Intent Integrity**: Did we solve the *original* problem, or a different one?
  2. **Tone Fidelity**: Did we maintain the required voice and empathy (e.g., Ruby Red)?
  3. **Innovation Relevance**: Are the added innovations relevant, or are they distracting rabbit holes?
