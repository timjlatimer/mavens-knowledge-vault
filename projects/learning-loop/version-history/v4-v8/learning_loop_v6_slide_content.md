## Slide 4: Phase 1 — System of Record (Correctness)
- **Headline**: Is the output good enough to deliver? (Score ≥ 80)
- **Key Points**:
  - SCORE → GAP → FIX → ACTION
  - Completeness, Clarity, Accuracy, Depth, Actionability

---

## Slide 5: Phase 2 — System of Innovation (Competitiveness)
- **Headline**: How can we make this even better? (External Scan)
- **Key Points**:
  - Mandatory "Best Practice Junkie" Innovation Scan
  - Clarity, Completeness, Impact, Elegance
  - Continue/Stop decision based on diminishing returns

---

## Slide 6: Phase 3 — System of Alignment (Integrity)
- **Headline**: Did we lose the spirit of the request? (The Guardian)
- **Key Points**:
  - A separate "Editor-in-Chief" persona
  - Compares final output against the **North Star**
  - Checks Intent, Tone, and Relevance

---

## Slide 7: Phase 4 — System of Communication (The Presenter) — THE NEW PHASE
- **Headline**: Is the output ready to share? (Automated Presentation)
- **Key Points**:
  - A mandatory final step after the Guardian's approval.
  - **Process**: Extract → Outline → Present
  - **Output**: A Swiss-style presentation deck summarizing the key findings.
