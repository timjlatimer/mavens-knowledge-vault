## What Is V13.0

The Learning Loop Protocol V13.0 is a universal nine-phase evaluation and certification system with a persistent learning agent. It ensures every AI-generated deliverable is Correct, Competitive, Aligned, Communicated, Evolutionary, and Certified. The defining innovation of V13.0 is **The Genie** — a persistent agent that stays in the user's environment, proactively offers to run the protocol at the end of every conversation, and tracks cumulative improvement over time.

The protocol is no longer something you invoke. It is something that lives with you.

## The Three-Layer Architecture

**Layer C (The Wrapper)**: The Genie IS the protocol. Installing V13.0 means installing a persistent presence that wraps everything — always aware, always watching, always ready to offer the protocol when it detects work that would benefit.

**Layer B (The Always-On Radar)**: The expanded Phase 7 (Drift Agent) scans the entire environment continuously — not just one deliverable, but every conversation, output, and decision — for alignment drift.

**Layer A (The Interaction Point)**: Phase 8 (The Genie's Offer) fires at the end of every protocol execution and every conversation. It presents a baseline score, projected improvement, and asks: "Would you like me to run the full Learning Loop?"

## The Persistence Engine

Six mandatory enforcement mechanisms prevent the protocol from drifting, skipping phases, or producing incomplete results:

**Phase Ledger**: A visible checklist created at start, updated after every phase, showing all 9 phases with scores and status. The single source of truth for protocol progress.

**One-Phase-Per-Response Rule**: Exactly one phase per response. Hard stop after each phase. Wait for human approval before proceeding. No exceptions.

**Phase Completion Proof**: Every phase ends with a structured output block showing phase-specific results, KPI delta, **North Star Alignment observation**, and running total. If you cannot produce this block with real data, the phase is not complete.

**Anti-Collapse Directive**: Explicit prohibitions against combining phases, summarizing instead of executing, skipping required outputs, using brevity language, or producing placeholder data.

**Mid-Protocol Self-Audit**: At Phase 4, verify Phases 0-3 were each executed properly as separate responses with real scores, that Phase 0 confirmed the North Star before scoring, that every Phase Completion Proof includes a North Star Alignment line, and that all KPI scores were calibrated through the North Star lens. Flag and remediate any gaps before continuing.

**End-of-Protocol Completeness Verification**: At Phase 8, verify every phase produced its required output, all presentations and documents were actually created, and the KPI journey is consistent.

## The Per-Phase KPI System

Every phase re-scores the deliverable across all 5 dimensions (Completeness, Clarity, Accuracy, Depth, Actionability — 20 points each) after applying its improvements. **All scores are calibrated through the confirmed North Star lens** — not generic quality, but fitness for the specific person the deliverable serves. The North Star shapes every dimension: Completeness = "covers what they need," Clarity = "they can understand it given their cognitive load," Accuracy = "relevant to their situation," Depth = "enough for them to act without overwhelming them," Actionability = "they can do this with the resources they actually have." The system tracks baseline, per-phase delta, running total, projected improvement, actual certified score, North Star alignment per phase, and cumulative improvement across all executions.

The Phase 6 certificate includes the complete improvement journey visualization showing each phase's point contribution. The Phase 8 Genie report includes cumulative data across all executions in the environment.

## Configuration

**North Star (MANDATORY CALIBRATION INSTRUMENT)**: Must be confirmed in Phase 0 before any scoring begins. The North Star is not optional configuration — it is the calibration instrument that gives the KPI system its meaning. Without a North Star, the KPI measures craftsmanship but not alignment. Default: Ruby Red (SIC Solve Team — 35-45 year old mom, CFO of household, working poor). Override with any persona, but always announce and confirm.

**Ethics Frameworks**: Purpose with Profit (default, social impact first), Profit with Purpose (financial viability first), Profit with Profit (pure financial optimization).

**Intensity Levels**: CASUAL (25%), ENGAGED (50%), DRIVEN (85% — default), PRIMAL (95%), PRIMAL+ (100%).

**Execution Modes**: Full Protocol (all 9 phases) or Quick Scan (Phase 0 + Phase 1 only, with projected improvement estimate).
