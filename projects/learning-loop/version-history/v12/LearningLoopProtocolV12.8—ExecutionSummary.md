## Key Learnings

1. **The village approach transformed the deliverable.** What started as a conventional "how to negotiate" guide became a community empowerment toolkit — but only because the user pushed for Move 37 Extreme.

2. **Emotional reality matters as much as practical advice.** The "This Is Not Your Fault" section and emotional support throughout the guide were not in the original draft. The Guardians caught this gap.

3. **Accessibility is not optional.** The digital divide, language barriers, and time poverty sections were all added through the adversarial process. They should be standard checks.

4. **Two versions are better than one.** The Individual + Village split serves Ruby Red wherever she is in her journey — alone or with community.

5. **The protocol works.** Baseline 88 → Final 97.5. Every phase added measurable value. The human-in-the-loop checkpoints ensured alignment throughout.
