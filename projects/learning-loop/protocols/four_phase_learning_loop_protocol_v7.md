# The Four-Phase Learning Loop Protocol (V7)
## With Integrated Smelling Salts Diagnostics

---

## **The Core Architecture: The Two-Speed Gearbox**

The V7 Protocol introduces a fundamental architectural shift. It recognizes that AI quality assurance happens at two distinct speeds:

1.  **Tactical Speed (Real-Time)**: The **Smelling Salts Protocol**. This is the "nervous system" of the AI, allowing for instant, mid-conversation course correction.
2.  **Strategic Speed (Post-Mortem)**: The **Learning Loop Protocol**. This is the "quality system" of the AI, ensuring that major deliverables meet the highest standards of correctness, competitiveness, and integrity.

---

## **The Symbiotic Link: Phase 3 Upgrade**

While the Smelling Salts operate independently during the conversation, their usage is now formally tracked and analyzed during the Learning Loop.

**Phase 3 (The Guardian)** has been upgraded to include a **Cognitive Health Check**:

> "Did the AI require the use of Smelling Salts (Red, White, Black, Gold) to produce this deliverable? If so, does this indicate a systemic failure in the AI's default mode that requires a permanent fix?"

This ensures that tactical interventions (Smelling Salts) are converted into strategic improvements (Learning Loop).

---

## **Phase 1: The System of Record (Correctness)**
*Engine A: The Baseline Quality Gate*

**Objective:** Ensure the output meets the basic requirements and is free of errors.

**The 5 Dimensions:**
1.  **Completeness:** Did we answer the whole question?
2.  **Clarity:** Is it easy to understand?
3.  **Accuracy:** Is it factually correct?
4.  **Depth:** Did we go deep enough?
5.  **Actionability:** Can the user do something with it?

**The Rule:** Score < 80/100 = FAIL. Fix immediately.

---

## **Phase 2: The System of Innovation (Competitiveness)**
*Engine B: The Best Practice Junkie*

**Objective:** Ensure the output is state-of-the-art and competitive with the best in the world.

**Step 1: The Innovation Scan (Mandatory)**
-   **Action:** Conduct a targeted "mini-research" sprint.
-   **Query:** "What is the state-of-the-art for [Output Type] in [Current Year]?"
-   **Goal:** Find 1-3 external "Gold Standards" to measure against.

**Step 2: The Iteration**
-   Identify the top 3 weaknesses against the Gold Standard.
-   Propose aggressive improvements.
-   Execute the changes.

---

## **Phase 3: The System of Alignment (Integrity)**
*The Guardian: The Spirit Check*

**Objective:** Ensure the output has not drifted from the original intent and maintains the correct voice.

**The Persona:** The "Editor-in-Chief" (blind to previous phases).

**The 4-Point Spirit Check:**
1.  **Intent Integrity:** Did we solve the *original* problem?
2.  **Tone Fidelity:** Did we maintain the "Ruby Red" empathy?
3.  **Innovation Relevance:** Are the innovations actually useful, or just shiny objects?
4.  **Cognitive Health (NEW):** Did we need Smelling Salts to get here? (If yes, flag for system review).

**The Rule:** One FAIL = REJECT. Return to Phase 1.

---

## **Phase 4: The System of Communication (Presentation)**
*The Presenter: The Swiss Package*

**Objective:** Ensure the deliverable is communicated with maximum clarity and elegance.

**The Standard:** Swiss International Style.
-   **Grid:** Strict mathematical alignment.
-   **Typography:** Sans-serif (Helvetica/Inter), high contrast, hierarchy.
-   **Whitespace:** Active use of negative space.
-   **Color:** Minimalist palette (Black, White, Red).

**The Action:** Automatically generate a presentation deck summarizing the key findings of the deliverable.
