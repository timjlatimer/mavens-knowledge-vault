# How to Negotiate a Lower Utility Bill
## A Quick Guide for Ruby Red

### Before You Call

1. **Gather your last 3 bills** - Know your average monthly cost
2. **Check competitor rates** - Look up what other providers charge in your area
3. **Note your payment history** - If you've been a good customer, mention it

### The Call Script

**Opening:**
> "Hi, I've been a customer for [X years] and I'm looking at my budget. I'd like to discuss options to lower my monthly bill."

**Key phrases to use:**
- "What programs do you have for customers who need assistance?"
- "Are there any discounts I might qualify for?"
- "I've seen lower rates from [competitor] - can you match that?"

### Programs to Ask About

- **Budget billing** - Spreads costs evenly across 12 months
- **Low-income assistance** - LIHEAP, utility company hardship programs
- **Senior/veteran discounts** - If applicable
- **Paperless billing discount** - Usually $2-5/month

### If They Say No

1. Ask to speak to a supervisor
2. Mention you're considering switching providers
3. Ask "What CAN you do for me today?"

### After the Call

- Get confirmation in writing
- Mark your calendar to call again in 6-12 months
- Rates change - always worth asking again

---

*Remember: The worst they can say is no. You lose nothing by asking.*
