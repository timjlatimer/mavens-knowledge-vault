# Lower Your Utility Bill: Individual Action Guide
## For Ruby Red - When You Need to Act NOW

*Estimated savings: $20-100/month | Time required: 30-60 minutes*

---

## 🎯 THE QUICK WIN (Do This Today)

### Step 1: Prepare (10 minutes)
- [ ] Find your last 3 utility bills
- [ ] Write down your average monthly cost
- [ ] Note how long you've been a customer
- [ ] Have a pen ready to write down names and confirmation numbers

### Step 2: Make the Call

**Opening Script:**
> "Hi, my name is [NAME]. I've been a customer for [X years] and I'm looking at my budget carefully. I'd like to discuss options to lower my monthly bill. Can you help me with that?"

**If they ask why:**
> "I'm a mom trying to make ends meet, and every dollar counts for my family right now."

**Key Questions to Ask:**
1. "What assistance programs do you have for customers in my situation?"
2. "Are there any discounts I might qualify for that I'm not currently receiving?"
3. "Can you review my rate plan to see if there's a better option for my usage?"
4. "Do you offer budget billing to even out my payments?"

### Step 3: Programs to Request By Name

| Program | What It Does | Typical Savings |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **LIHEAP** | Federal assistance for heating/cooling | Up to $500/year |
| **Budget Billing** | Averages your bill across 12 months | Predictability |
| **Low-Income Rate** | Reduced rate for qualifying households | 20-35% off |
| **Medical Baseline** | Extra allowance for medical equipment | Varies |
| **Paperless + AutoPay** | Discounts for electronic billing | $5-10/month |

### Step 4: If They Say No

**Escalation Script:**
> "I understand. Before I go, can I speak with a supervisor? I want to make sure I've explored every option available to me."

**The Competitor Card:**
> "I've been looking at [competitor name] and their rates are lower. Is there anything you can do to keep me as a customer?"

**The Last Ask:**
> "I appreciate your time. What CAN you do for me today, even if it's small?"

---

## 📝 AFTER THE CALL

- [ ] Write down the name of everyone you spoke with
- [ ] Get a confirmation number for any changes
- [ ] Request confirmation in writing (email or mail)
- [ ] Set a calendar reminder to call again in 6 months
- [ ] Check your next bill to verify changes were applied

---

## 🔧 QUICK WINS WHILE YOU WAIT

**Free, immediate actions:**
- Unplug phone chargers and small appliances when not in use
- Switch to cold water for laundry
- Lower water heater to 120°F
- Replace one incandescent bulb with LED ($1 at dollar store)
- Close vents in unused rooms
