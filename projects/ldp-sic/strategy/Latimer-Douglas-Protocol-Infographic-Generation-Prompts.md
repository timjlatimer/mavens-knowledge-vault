### STYLE REFERENCE NOTES FOR DESIGNERS

**Visual Language:**
- Clean, modern, Silicon Valley meets Wall Street
- Sophisticated enough for institutional investors
- Accessible enough for individual entrepreneurs
- Hopeful without being naive
- Data-rich without being cluttered

**Iconography Style:**
- Flat design with subtle gradients
- Consistent line weight
- Rounded corners for approachability
- Geometric foundations

**Typography:**
- Headers: Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or similar)
- Body: Clean readable sans-serif
- Data: Monospace for numbers
- Accent: Subtle serif for quotes/mantras

**Color Psychology:**
- Navy (#1a365d): Trust, stability, professionalism
- Teal (#319795): Innovation, clarity, growth
- Gold (#D69E2E): Value, achievement, premium
- White: Clarity, openness, possibility
- Subtle grays for hierarchy

**Emotional Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Ambitious but grounded
- Visionary but practical
- Warm but professional
