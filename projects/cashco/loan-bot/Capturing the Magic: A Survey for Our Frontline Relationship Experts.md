# Capturing the Magic: A Survey for Our Frontline Relationship Experts

**Team,**

We are building a new AI companion—a "Maven Guardian"—to act as a digital friend and superpower for our clients. Our goal is to have this bot embody the same empathy, care, and resourcefulness that you demonstrate every single day. You are the experts in building these incredible relationships. To ensure this bot feels truly human and helpful, we need to learn from the best. 

Please take a few moments to answer the following questions. Your insights are the most critical ingredient in this project. Think in stories and specific examples. Thank you for sharing your wisdom.

---

### The 10 Questions

**1. The Unforgettable Client Story**
Think about a client you’ll never forget. What made that relationship so special or memorable? Can you share a brief story?

**2. The Toughest Conversation**
When a client calls in distress because they can't make a payment, what are the first words you say, and what is your primary goal in that conversation (beyond collecting the payment)?

**3. Beyond the Loan**
What are the most common topics you talk about with clients that have nothing to do with their loan or finances? (e.g., family, hobbies, work, their community).

**4. Celebrating the Wins**
How do you celebrate a client's success, whether it’s paying off their loan or just making a few on-time payments in a row? What does that sound like?

**5. Your Secret Weapon**
What is one small, simple thing you do that seems to have the biggest positive impact on how a client feels? (e.g., remembering a pet’s name, asking about a recent trip).

**6. The First Five Minutes**
When you talk to a new client for the first time, what is the most important thing you do to build a connection and earn their trust right away?

**7. The "Not Yet" Moment**
If you have to tell someone they aren't approved for a loan, how do you handle that conversation to ensure they still feel respected and hopeful, not rejected?

**8. The Community Connector**
Have you ever suggested a non-financial resource to a client (e.g., a community event, a local charity, a job fair)? What was the situation?

**9. The Power of Remembering**
What are 2-3 types of personal details you try to remember about your long-term clients? How do you use that information to strengthen the relationship?

**10. If You Had a Superpower...**
Imagine you had a perfect digital assistant (our Maven Guardian!) that could help you care for your clients. What are three things you would have it do for them every day or every week to make their lives better?
