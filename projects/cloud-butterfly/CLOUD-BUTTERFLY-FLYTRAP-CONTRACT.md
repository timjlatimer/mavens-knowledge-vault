## 8. What Happens After Submission

Once a butterfly lands in Mission Control, here's the lifecycle:

1. **Submitted** — Appears in the Cloud Butterfly Gallery with animated wings
2. **Reviewed** — A team member reads and acknowledges it
3. **Categorized** — Assigned to an initiative and/or ring
4. **Promoted** — Elevated to the News Channel or highlighted in the 3D Diamond
5. **Archived** — Processed and stored for historical reference

The Flytrap app does NOT need to manage this lifecycle. Submit and forget. Mission Control handles the rest.
