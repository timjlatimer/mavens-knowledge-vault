# Research Notes: HB1000, Cloud Butterflies, and Moonshots

## HB1000 — The Hive Mind
- HB1000 = "Hive Brain 1000" — the user's term for the collective intelligence of the SIC Solve Team
- It represents the human-in-the-loop input engine — the wisdom of the team members
- Each team member is a "wisdom giant" in their area of expertise
- The HB1000 is the human side of the human-AI partnership
- Pearl needs "Flypaper" — the input engine that captures HB1000 insights from the field
- The concept: Pearl + HB1000 together create superpowers that neither can achieve alone

## Cloud Butterflies
- The user's term for spontaneous ideation sessions — stream-of-consciousness brainstorming
- Named for the ephemeral, floating nature of ideas that need to be captured before they fly away
- Cloud Butterflies are dictated (voice-first), not written — they come in raw, unstructured form
- They often contain repetition, tangents, and overlapping concepts that need deduplication
- Pearl's job: capture, deduplicate, organize, and act on Cloud Butterflies
- The "record button" concept — always-on capture of ideas from meetings, conversations, walks

## Moonshots (Diamandis Framework)
- A Moonshot = 10X improvement, not 10% improvement
- "The day before something is a breakthrough, it's a crazy idea"
- Three reasons for 10X over 10%:
  1. 10% puts you in direct competition with everyone else
  2. 10X forces you to approach the problem completely differently
  3. Treating any problem as solvable leads to shocking breakthroughs
- Massively Transformative Purpose (MTP) — the guiding purpose behind the moonshot
- In the AI era (per user): BHAG horizon compressed from 10-25 years to 1-2 years
- Moonshots must be: Positive-Sum, Auditable, Composable (from Solve Everything)

## The User's Vision for Moonshots in Pearl
- A dedicated Moonshot space/tab within Pearl
- Users can pick from Diamandis/Wissner-Gross moonshots OR define their own
- "Bingo carding" on moonshots — visual grid of moonshot options
- Build out moonshots with full canvas/planning tools
- Incentive: "If they do a moonshot, we will pay for half their token use inside the moonshot canvas area"
- Reference the Solve Everything paper's 9-10 chapters of moonshots within Pearl
