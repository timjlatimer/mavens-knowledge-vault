# HB1000 Cultural Universality — Critical Design Requirement

## The Core Insight
The HB1000 is NOT a Western self-help framework. It is a universal operating system for being human that must:
- Speak multiple languages
- Understand multiple cultures
- Understand multiple clichés/proverbs from every tradition
- Work for Ruby Red whether she's in Calgary, Lagos, Mumbai, São Paulo, or Manila

## What This Means for Architecture
- The cliché/wisdom library must be multi-cultural from day one
- The setup wizard must be culturally adaptive
- The frameworks we draw from must include non-Western traditions
- The language of the system must be translatable without losing meaning
- "Best practice junkie" behaviour means best practices from ALL cultures

## Research Needed
- Cross-cultural proverb studies
- Universal human values research (Schwartz values, World Values Survey)
- Non-Western life management frameworks (Ikigai, Ubuntu, Dharma, etc.)
- How wisdom traditions differ AND converge across cultures
- Multilingual proverb databases
