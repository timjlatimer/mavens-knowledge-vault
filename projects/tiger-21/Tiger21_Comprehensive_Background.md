## THE CORNER BANKER / GUARDIAN SYSTEM

**The AI-powered "nervous system" that connects all three legs of the stool**

The Corner Banker (also called "The Guardian") is an AI-driven financial protection and advocacy system operating on the "Big Data Flip" principle: using community intelligence to rescue clients rather than exploit them.

### Target Customer
"The Wallet Warrior" — the 35-45 year old CFO of the household navigating life on thin margins

### Core Functions

**1. NSF Prediction & Prevention (The Shield)**

- 90-day trailing budget analysis
- Predicts NSF 2-3 days BEFORE it hits

- Shows "Safe to Spend" number (not just balance)

## GIVE LIKE A BILLIONAIRE INITIATIVE

### The Core Concept
Banks "have an affair" with your money every night—taking idle deposits, lending them overnight in the money market, earning interest, and giving you $0. Give Like a Billionaire turns that invisible extraction into visible community good.

### How It Works
1. Members grant Maven access to move idle funds (~$1,500 avg) to holding account overnight
2. Maven uses same fractional reserve rules banks use
3. Funds earn overnight interest (~2.5% annually / 0.0069% daily)
4. Interest funds Maven operations OR direct community benefits
5. Member's principal returned next day (or within 3 days if needed)
6. Reserve kept for anyone who needs funds immediately
7. Member has 100% instant access to funds at all times
