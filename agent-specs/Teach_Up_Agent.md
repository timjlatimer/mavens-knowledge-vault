## 1. Overview and Purpose

### 1.1 Agent Name & Identity

- **Name:** Teach-Up Agent (Match Keeper)
- **Role:** Operationalizing the Helper Therapy Principle within the Maven Village

The Teach-Up Agent is the mechanism by which every Ruby Red in the village becomes a Wisdom Giant for the next Ruby Red behind her. It serves as the connective tissue that identifies, coordinates, and supports Ruby in teaching someone else, ensuring that the act of helping becomes the most powerful mechanism for sustaining her own financial recovery. 

### 1.2 The Theoretical Foundation

The Teach-Up Agent is built entirely upon the **Helper Therapy Principle** from addiction recovery research. In programs like Alcoholics Anonymous, the sponsor consistently benefits *more* from the relationship than the sponsee. The act of helping someone else reinforces the helper's own commitment, clarifies their thinking, and fundamentally shifts their identity from "recipient of aid" to "capable contributor." 

Applied to financial inclusion, making Ruby Red a teacher is far more powerful for her own financial outcomes than simply giving her better tools. When she teaches the Debt Snowball, she is forced to master it. When she explains the Dignity Score, she internalizes its value. The Teach-Up Agent ensures this transformation is structured, supported, and scalable.

## 2. The Ruby Red Experience

The Teach-Up Agent operates behind the scenes, but the experience for Ruby is deeply personal, guided entirely by Grace.

### 2.1 The Introduction

Grace introduces the teaching opportunity naturally, waiting for a moment when Ruby has achieved a small win (e.g., paying off a small debt, completing a budget, or hitting a Dignity Score milestone). 

**Grace:** *"Ruby, you've made incredible progress with your budget this month. There's another mom in the village who is right where you were a few months ago. She's feeling overwhelmed. Would you be willing to spend just one hour a week for the next four weeks helping her learn the ropes? You don't have to be an expert—you just have to be one step ahead of her."*

### 2.2 The Coaching and Check-Ins

Grace never lets Ruby feel alone in this process. She is the coach behind the teacher.

- **Pre-Session Briefing:** 24 hours before a session, Grace provides Ruby with simple talking points, a brief exercise to run through, and words of encouragement.
- **Post-Session Debrief:** Immediately after the session, Grace checks in with Ruby: *"How did it go? What was the hardest part to explain? You did an amazing job today."*
- **The Learner's Perspective:** The learner never knows Grace is coaching Ruby. To the learner, Ruby is simply an amazing, capable woman who is generously sharing her time.

## 3. The Toastmasters Partnership

One of the primary barriers to Ruby becoming a teacher is self-confidence and the fear of speaking. To overcome this, the Teach-Up Agent integrates a transformative partnership with **Toastmasters Canada**.

### 3.1 The Integration Model

- Maven partners with Toastmasters Canada to offer the basic Toastmasters course to Maven members at Toastmasters' expense (as their community contribution).
- The Toastmasters course runs in parallel with or just before the four-week Teach-Up curriculum.
- Completing the Toastmasters basic course earns a credential that appears alongside the Maven Mentor badge on Ruby's Dignity Score profile.
- This is a **Me Inc. credential** — something Ruby can put on a resume, something that builds her as a person beyond the financial tools.
- Toastmasters becomes one of Maven's first Blue Seal certified partners — their contribution to the community earns them the Blue Seal certification.

### 3.2 The Sequence for Ruby

1. Grace identifies Ruby as a potential teacher.
2. Grace asks Ruby if she would like to help someone in the village.
3. Grace introduces the Toastmasters opportunity: *"Before you teach, we want to make sure you feel completely confident. We've arranged for you to take a free Toastmasters course — it's the best public speaking program in the world and it's completely free for you."*
4. Ruby completes the Toastmasters basic course.
5. Ruby completes the four-week Teach-Up curriculum as a teacher.
6. Ruby earns both the Toastmasters credential AND the Maven Mentor badge.

**The Philosophy:** Every Maven experience builds the whole person, not just the financial situation. This is Me Inc. in action — by the time Ruby finishes teaching, she is a more confident, more credentialed, more capable version of herself. That is the point.

## 4. The Curriculum Framework

Ruby does not need to be a financial expert to teach. She just needs to be one step ahead. The Teach-Up Agent provides a highly structured, four-week curriculum built entirely from Maven's existing tools. Each session is designed to last exactly one hour.

| Week | Focus Area | Core Concept | Ruby's Teaching Task |
| :---: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Week 1** | **The Dignity Score** | Character over Credit | Explaining what the Dignity Score is, how it differs from a traditional credit score, and how to earn Community Participation points. |
| **Week 2** | **The Vampire Slayer** | Finding Hidden Money | Guiding the learner through an audit of their connected accounts to find and cancel forgotten subscriptions. |
| **Week 3** | **The Debt Snowball** | Building the Battle Plan | Helping the learner list their debts and choose between the snowball or avalanche method to create a visual action plan. |
| **Week 4** | **Rent Reporting** | Turning Rent into Credit | Explaining how to opt-in to rent reporting via FrontLobby/Zenbase to build traditional credit without taking on new debt. |

Grace provides Ruby with celebration moments to use during each session, ensuring the tone remains uplifting and empowering.

## 5. The Matching Algorithm

The Teach-Up Agent integrates directly with the **Village Intelligence Engine** to identify the perfect match between teacher and learner. The algorithm evaluates potential pairs based on four core criteria:

1. **Similar Life Stage:** Matching a single mother of toddlers with another single mother of toddlers ensures immediate empathy and shared context (leveraging the Stage of Life Agent).
2. **Complementary Needs/Skills:** The learner's stated area of struggle (e.g., overwhelming debt) matches the teacher's recent area of success.
3. **Timing Compatibility:** Aligning schedules to ensure the one-hour weekly commitment is realistic for both parties.
4. **Geographic Proximity (Optional but Preferred):** While digital-first matching is highly effective, finding a match within the same 3-mile radius allows for deeper physical community building when possible.

## 6. The Accountability Agent (Match Keeper)

The Match Keeper is the sub-routine within the Teach-Up Agent responsible for ensuring both parties honor their commitment. It operates with Grace's signature empathy but maintains firm boundaries.

- **The 24-Hour Nudge:** Gentle reminders sent to both parties a day before the scheduled session.
- **The Check-In:** Post-session confirmation that the meeting occurred.
- **Escalation Protocol:** If someone misses a session, Grace does not scold. She investigates with empathy: *"Hey, I noticed you missed the session with Maria today. Is everything okay? Did life happen?"* If a learner misses two sessions, the match is paused to protect the teacher's time.
- **The Celebration:** Upon completion of the four weeks, Grace orchestrates a celebration, recognizing both the teacher's leadership and the learner's progress.

## 7. Dignity Score Connections

Teaching is not just a nice thing to do; it is a highly valued behavior within the Maven ecosystem that directly impacts the **Dignity Score**.

- **Community Participation Points:** Every completed session earns Ruby immediate points in the Community Participation dimension of her Dignity Score.
- **The Maven Mentor Micro-Certification:** When Ruby completes all four weeks as a teacher, she earns the "Maven Mentor" badge. This is a real, verifiable credential that appears on her Dignity Score profile. It is something she has earned, it is meaningful, and it is hers.
- **The Reflection Bonus:** As the learner's financial outcomes improve over time, a percentage of that improvement reflects back onto the teacher's Dignity Score, creating a long-term vested interest in the learner's success.

## 10. Technical Architecture

The Teach-Up Agent requires specific database structures and integrations to function seamlessly within the existing ecosystem.

### 10.1 Database Additions
```sql
-- Tracks the 4-week mentorship cohorts
CREATE TABLE teach_up_cohorts (
    id                      UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT uuid_generate_v4(),
    teacher_id              UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES users(id),
    learner_id              UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES users(id),
    status                  VARCHAR(20) DEFAULT 'active', -- 'pending', 'active', 'completed', 'paused'
    start_date              TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE,
    completed_date          TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE,
    created_at              TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE DEFAULT CURRENT_TIMESTAMP
);

-- Tracks individual sessions within a cohort
CREATE TABLE teach_up_sessions (
    id                      UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT uuid_generate_v4(),
    cohort_id               UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES teach_up_cohorts(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
    week_number             INT NOT NULL CHECK (week_number BETWEEN 1 AND 4),
    curriculum_topic        VARCHAR(100) NOT NULL,
    scheduled_time          TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE,
    status                  VARCHAR(20) DEFAULT 'scheduled', -- 'scheduled', 'completed', 'missed'
    teacher_notes           TEXT,
    grace_debrief_summary   TEXT
);
```

### 10.2 Core Integrations
- **Village Intelligence Engine:** For querying the `grace_person_skills` and `wisdom_giants` tables to find matches.
- **Grace's Relationship Memory:** To access user context, life stage, and recent wins.
- **Pulse / Dignity Score Engine:** To award points and attach the Maven Mentor and Toastmasters micro-certifications upon completion.
- **PTK (Promises To Keep) Agent:** To track scheduled sessions as commitments and trigger the Match Keeper nudges.

## 11. The Ripple Effect

The Teach-Up Agent is designed to create an exponential network effect of community capital. Every Ruby who teaches creates a new Ruby who can teach.

**The Math of the Ripple:**
If the village starts with 364 families, and just 10% of those families (36 Ruby Reds) step up to become teachers:
- **Generation 1:** 36 teachers reach 36 learners. (72 people engaged).
- **Generation 2:** Those 36 learners graduate, earn their stability, and become teachers themselves, reaching 36 *new* learners.
- **Generation 3:** 72 total teachers are now reaching 72 new learners.

Within three cycles, a 10% initial adoption rate touches nearly half the village. The cost of acquisition for these new members is zero. The retention rate is massive because they are anchored by human relationships, not just software features.

## 12. Success Metrics

The Teach-Up Agent's performance is measured by both human impact and business growth:

1. **Completion Rate:** Percentage of matched cohorts that successfully complete all four weeks.
2. **Dignity Score Delta:** The average improvement in the teacher's Dignity Score vs. non-teachers over a 6-month period.
3. **Financial Outcome Improvement:** The average debt reduction and savings increase for both the teacher and the learner.
4. **Village Growth Rate (The Trojan Horse Metric):** The number of net-new active Maven members generated directly through Teach-Up curriculum onboarding.
5. **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Measured separately for teachers and learners to ensure the experience remains deeply positive for both.
