# Milk Money Communications Library

## 1. What Is Milk Money
Milk Money is a small pool of community-funded dollars created to help Maven members through those tight, in-between moments. Whether it is gas in the tank or groceries before payday, this support is here to offer a little breathing room. It is simple, built on trust, and sustained by members who repay so the next person can be supported too. 💛

## 2. Program Parameters
- **Starting Limit:** $25.00 for new members.
- **Maximum Limit:** Members can work their way up to accessing $100.00 with successful repayment history.
- **Repayment Terms:** For the initial $25.00 limit, reimbursement is split into two payments of $14.99 back to the community pool.
- **Eligibility:** Members must be in good standing and agree to contribute back to the pool to ensure funds remain available for others.

### Template 1: Program Introduction Email
**Subject:** Introducing Milk Money: A Little Help, Right When You Need It

Hi Maven Community,

Let’s talk about real life for a moment.

It is the gas light glowing two days before payday.
It is opening the pantry and realizing the lunch kits are missing just one more snack.
It is that tiny gap between “almost there” and “not quite yet.”

That is where Milk Money comes in.

Milk Money is a small, community-powered pool of funds available to Maven members who find themselves needing a few extra dollars to bridge a short-term gap. Nothing dramatic. Nothing complicated. Just practical support for everyday moments.

We are currently in the testing phase of this support option, and we are keeping it intentionally simple. Our goal is to make these funds accessible with few to no hoops to jump through. If you need help, we want the process to feel human, not bureaucratic.

Here is how it works:

To access Milk Money, you simply need to:
- Be a Maven member in good standing.
- Contribute back to the pool when you are able, so the next person can access support when they need it.

That is it.

Milk Money is built on trust, reciprocity, and the belief that small acts of support can create steady ground beneath all of us. Sometimes you are the one reaching in. Sometimes you are the one refilling the jar. Both matter.

We are learning as we go, and your participation and feedback will help shape how this grows. Our hope is that Milk Money becomes a quiet safety net woven by the community itself.

If you find yourself needing a little help, please know that this option exists for you.

With care,
The Maven Team

## 5. Brand Voice Guidelines
When communicating about Milk Money, all messaging must adhere to these key tone notes:
- **Empathetic & Human:** The tone must be deeply empathetic, acknowledging the reality of living paycheck to paycheck without judgment. It should never sound "businessy" or corporate.
- **Light & Approachable:** Keep the language simple and light. Avoid heavy or overly formal terms.
- **Community-First:** Emphasize the collective nature of the fund. It is about members supporting members, creating a ripple effect of care.
- **Urgency Without Pressure:** When following up on repayments, create a sense of urgency by explaining *why* the funds are needed (to help the next person), but avoid sounding mean, demanding, or overly punitive.
