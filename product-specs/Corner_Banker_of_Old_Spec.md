# The Corner Banker of Old™ — Product Specification

## 1. Executive Summary

The Corner Banker of Old is the second leg of the SIC Solve Team's Three-Legged Stool architecture, designed to provide immediate cash-flow relief and micro-funding for the working poor. It operates under the philosophy that "It's expensive to be poor. We're here to fix that."

This product is not a replacement for a traditional bank; it is positioned as "Private Banking for the Working Poor"—a financial consultant overlay that sits on top of the client's existing banking relationship. The Corner Banker of Old is a metaphor and persona, not a corporate entity, and provides tools that empower the household CFO (our "Ruby Red" persona) to navigate the political, bureaucratic, and greedy capitalist worlds with dignity.

### The Three-Legged Stool Architecture
*   **Leg 1: Maven** — Community, hand-ups, daily supports, credit support.
*   **Leg 2: Corner Banker / Money Up** — Micro-funding, cash-flow relief, instant-access tools.
*   **Leg 3: Pulse** — Credit rebuilding, reporting, financial insights.

## 3. Micro-Loan Mechanics (Need Milk Money)

The "Need Milk Money" (NMM) feature provides instant access to small amounts of capital to solve immediate, low-cost crises (e.g., a flat tire, a school field trip) without resorting to predatory lending.

### Funding Parameters
*   **Loan Amounts:** Up to $100, available in $20 increments ($20, $40, $60, $80, $100).
*   **Interest Rate:** 9.95% APR (translating to pennies on a $100 loan).
*   **Repayment Structure:** Split evenly across the next two Fridays (e.g., $50 and $50 for a $100 loan).

### Graduated Trust System
Access to higher tiers is earned through successful repayment behavior, reinforcing the Dignity Score.
*   **Rookie:** $20 limit
*   **Starter:** $40 limit
*   **Reliable:** $60 limit
*   **Trusted:** $100 limit
*   **Elite:** $150 limit (Unlocked after 12 consecutive on-time repayments)

### Access Methods & Fees
*   **Instant Access:** $4.95 EMT (Email Money Transfer) fee. (Note: Hard cost to Maven is $1.50. Anticipated 95% adoption rate).
*   **Standard Access:** Next-day EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer). No fee.

## 4. Safety Net Protocol (No Collections — Ever)

The Corner Banker operates on a strict "No Collections" policy. When a client struggles, the system does not punish them; it slows down. This is the Safety Net Protocol.

*   **Level 0 (Standard):** Standard repayment schedule (e.g., $50 on each of the next two Fridays).
*   **Level 1 (Safety Step):** Triggered by a missed payment. The repayment automatically switches to $6/week ($5 principal + $1 service fee).
*   **Level 2 (Nano-Step):** Triggered if Level 1 fails. The repayment automatically switches to $3/week ($2 principal + $1 service fee).
*   **Service Suspension:** While in Level 1 or Level 2, further NMM service is suspended until the balance is cleared. Once cleared, service is immediately restored.
*   **Core Philosophy:** "We don't cut you off, we slow down." No collection calls are ever made.

## 5. Lifeline Link (Village Insurance) & The Gratitude Loop

The Corner Banker integrates community support directly into the financial model, allowing the "Village" to protect its own.

### Lifeline Link
*   **Mechanic:** Healthy members can choose to "Clear a Step" for a struggling neighbor by paying their $3 or $6 Nano-Step/Safety Step for the week.
*   **Visibility:** The "Village Wall" displays anonymized profiles of neighbors with brief story snippets explaining their situation.
*   **Giving Options:** Members can give anonymously or named. They can select a specific neighbor or contribute to the "Village Reserve Pool" for general distribution.
*   **Checkout Nudge:** Every time a member successfully makes a payment, they receive a gentle prompt offering the opportunity to clear a neighbor's step.

### Gratitude Loop
*   **Notification:** The recipient receives a warm notification: "A neighbor cleared your step."
*   **Response:** The recipient has a one-tap option to "Send a Heart" or draft a personal message.
*   **JOY Genie:** An AI assistant drafts 3 authentic thank-you options using the JOY formula (Gratitude, Modesty, Selflessness) to reduce the cognitive load of expressing thanks.
*   **Blind Relay:** Maven acts as the intermediary. The message is delivered, but neither party receives the other's contact information, preserving privacy and dignity.
