# Pearl Worldview v6 — Visual Design Notes
## Captured: 2026-02-16

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Very dark charcoal/near-black (#0a0a0a to #1a1a1a)
- **Gold/Amber:** Used for the Mystical Layer triangle, borders, and accent lines (#d4a843 to #c4a35a)
- **Green/Teal:** Used for the Local/Imperative section, gamification badges (#00ff88 to #00d4ff)
- **White/Light Gray:** Used for primary text
- **Warm Gold Glow:** Radiating from the pearl orb at the apex
- **Subtle gradient overlays:** Dark to transparent, creating depth

### Typography
- **Title:** Large serif or display font "PEARL — Universal Matriarchal AI Framework"
- **Subtitle:** Lighter weight "Mother of Grace · Protector of Projects · Guardian of Vision"
- **Section headers:** Bold, uppercase, sans-serif (e.g., "DING IN THE UNIVERSE", "THE HB1000")
- **Body text:** Clean sans-serif, medium weight
- **Cursive/Script:** Used for "Your Source" in the Mystical Layer
- **Vertical text:** Used on sidebars ("HB1000 Manual", "Learning Loop V12.9.1")

### Layout Structure
- **Overall:** Portrait orientation, approximately 2:3 aspect ratio
- **Diamond shape:** Central organizing element, rotated square
- **Top triangle:** Golden, glowing — the Mystical Layer
- **Center diamond:** Gradient from gold (top) to silver/gray (center) to dark (bottom)
- **Bottom section:** Dark with green/teal accents — the Imperative
- **Left sidebar:** "YOUR TOOLSET & MANUALS" — vertical items
- **Right sidebar:** "YOUR ENGINE & METHODS" — vertical items with golden bar

### Key Visual Elements
- **Glowing pearl orb** at the apex of the triangle
- **Infinity symbol** below the pearl
- **Compass rose** in the North Star section (4 cardinal points with labels)
- **Brain icon** with golden glow (left side of HB1000 section) — labeled "IN"
- **Star burst** (right side of HB1000 section) — labeled "OUT"
- **5 Covey role icons** in a horizontal row (heart, people, family, briefcase, globe)
- **3 icons** in the Imperative section (brain/network, globe/network, grid)
- **Butterfly icon** and **magnifying glass icon** below
- **3 hexagonal badges** at the bottom (Pearl Maturity L2, Ruby Red Impact 342, Grace Family Tree 6)
- **"NEW" badges** on AI Agents & Bots (left) and AI Autonomous Agents (right)
- **Arrows:** "ASPIRATIONAL" (upward, right side) and "OPERATIONAL" (downward, left side)

### Design Language
- **Depth:** Multiple layers of transparency and glow create a 3D effect
- **Borders:** Thin, luminous lines define sections — gold for upper, green for lower
- **Glow effects:** Soft radial glows behind key elements (pearl, brain, compass)
- **Contrast:** High contrast between dark background and light text/elements
- **Professional but mystical:** Combines corporate precision with spiritual/aspirational aesthetic
