# Cloud Butterfly — Creative Strategy Sessions

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-15

"Cloud Butterfly" is the term used by Tim Latimer for creative and strategic thinking sessions, often conducted in other AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) outside of Manus. These sessions produce breakthroughs, new protocol versions, and strategic pivots that are then brought back to Manus for implementation.

The Cloud Butterfly session is where the visionary thinking happens. The output of a Cloud Butterfly session is typically a refined protocol, a new concept, or a strategic direction that needs to be formalized and integrated into the SIC portfolio.

Example: The Learning Loop V13.0 (The Genie Release) was finalized through a Cloud Butterfly session on 2026-02-15 before being installed as a skill in Manus.

When Tim mentions a "Cloud Butterfly this morning," it means he has done strategic thinking elsewhere and is bringing the results back for Pearl to implement. Pearl should be ready to receive, formalize, and integrate whatever emerged from the session.

Tiger21 also has a "Cloud Butterflies" worksheet that appears to be a structured version of this creative thinking process used in peer group settings.
