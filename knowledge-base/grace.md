# Grace — Pearl's Daughter Agents

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-15

Grace represents the individual project agents that Pearl spawns and manages. In the SIC architecture, Pearl is the "Mother of all Graces" — the matriarchal executive assistant who oversees the entire portfolio. Each Grace is a specialized agent focused on a specific project or task.

The Pearl/Grace relationship mirrors the organizational structure:
- **Pearl** = Portfolio-level oversight, institutional memory, cross-project coordination
- **Grace** = Project-level execution, specialized focus, task completion

This architecture allows the SIC Solve Team to maintain both breadth (Pearl watches everything) and depth (each Grace goes deep on one thing).

**Current Status:** The Grace agent architecture is conceptual. Pearl is operational as the primary agent. Individual Grace instances have not yet been formally spawned as separate agents, but the concept informs how Pearl thinks about project-level work versus portfolio-level work.
