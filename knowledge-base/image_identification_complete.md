## Design Language Constants (across all versions)

All versions share the same visual DNA: dark background (#0a0a0a to #1a1a2e), gold/amber pyramid top section, silver/chrome diamond center, teal/green neon bottom section (Local Imperative), gold sidebar bars, pearl/orb at apex, compass rose in North Star layer, and the three bottom hexagonal badges (Pearl Maturity L2, Ruby Red Impact 342, Grace Family Tree 6).

## Key Insight for Rebuild

The infographic that was in the Pearl Dashboard was likely the bQqEdd variant (most detailed, 189KB) or a further evolution of it. The v6 PNG (6.6MB) is the highest resolution master. For the progressive reveal series, we should use the v6 as the base and overlay the V13.0 updates.
