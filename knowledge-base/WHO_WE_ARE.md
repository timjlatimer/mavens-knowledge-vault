## Ruby Red — Who We Solve For

Ruby Red is the **Chief Financial Officer of the household**. She is typically a 35-45 year old mother of two who is trying to figure out how to make her finances stretch until the next payday. She is part of the working poor, left out and left behind, the unbanked and underbanked part of the community.

**Her daily decisions:**
- Does she put something back when she's in the grocery line?
- Does she let the kids go to a $30 extracurricular event?
- How does she pay for the flat tire on the car?

**The guiding principle:** "It's expensive to be poor." We think that's a crime in society, and we're trying to change it.

**The superpower:** Empathy. "There but for the grace of God go I."

**The JTBD (Job To Be Done):** Make life work better for Ruby Red in three "gangster worlds":
1. **The Political World** — learned helplessness imposed by political systems
2. **The Bureaucratic World** — cognitive punishment for being poor (forms, wait times, complexity)
3. **The Greedy Capitalist World** — extracting maximum value from minimum resources

**The science behind Ruby Red:**
- Poverty is a cognitive condition, not just a financial one (Mullainathan & Shafir)
- Scarcity reduces cognitive bandwidth by 13-14 IQ points
- Every feature must RESTORE bandwidth, not consume it

**Ruby Red's JTBD at the emotional level:**
- Feel seen
- Feel less alone
- Feel capable
- Feel connected
- Start the day with intention

These are survival needs, not entertainment needs.

## The SIC Matrix

The Social Impact Capitalism Matrix measures organizations on two axes:

- **X-axis:** Economic Engine Strength (Failing → Thriving)
- **Y-axis:** Purpose Alignment & Impact (Extractive → Regenerative)

**Four zones:**
- **Deadwood Zone:** Failing on both counts
- **Martyr Zone:** High heart, no fuel — noble mission but broken economic engine
- **SOB Zone:** Traditional Extractive Capitalist — economically skilled but socially toxic
- **Honeybee Zone (THE SWEET SPOT at 7-8):** Profit and purpose as force multipliers

**Two paths to the Honeybee Zone:**
- "Build the People, They'll Build the Engine" (Martyr → Honeybee)
- "Adopt Purpose" (SOB → Honeybee)

The **Bee Chart** is the assessment tool for measuring where you sit on the matrix.
