## Maven's Six Pricing Principles

Maven's pricing is not a revenue model. It is a **relationship model** that happens to involve money. Every pricing decision flows from six principles:

| # | Principle | What It Means |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | **Grace Never Gets Cut** | Regardless of tier — including Free — Grace is always there. Full voice, full text, full memory, full empathy. Grace is not a feature. Grace is the relationship. |
| 2 | **Nobody Gets Abandoned** | When a payment fails, Maven does not lock the door. Maven opens a smaller door. And then a smaller one. And if all fails: "You're still a member of the Village. Grace is still here." No collections. No penalty. No shame. |
| 3 | **Trust, Not Contract** | No signatures. No commitments. No fine print. Cancel anytime. Change tiers anytime. Leave and come back. |
| 4 | **Cash-Flow Friendly** | Five payment intervals (monthly, weekly, weekly lite, daily, free) so Ruby Red can match payment to her cash flow. |
| 5 | **Step-Down Is a Feature** | When Ruby Red steps down, the system is working as designed. The step-down protects her from cascading consequences (overdraft fees, credit damage). Maven absorbs the disruption. |
| 6 | **Need Milk Money Is a Hand Up** | NMM is not charity. It is a micro-lending program. Ruby Red agrees to repay. Repayment has its own step-down. $2.50 admin fee covers costs. No interest. No penalties. No collections. |

## Need Milk Money (NMM)

Need Milk Money replaced the V1 "Community Chest" model. It is a micro-lending program controlled by Grace and Big Mama.

NMM allotments come in three sizes: $25, $50, or $100. The administration fee is $2.50 per allotment (covers the cost of moving the money). There are no interest charges, no penalties, and no collections. If Ruby Red cannot repay, the step-down logic absorbs it gracefully.

## The Puppet Strings Report

The Puppet Strings Report measures manipulation exposure across six dimensions:

| Dimension | What It Measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Fear | External fear-based manipulation |
| Scarcity | Artificial scarcity pressure |
| Social Proof | Herd behavior exploitation |
| Authority Bias | False authority claims |
| Harvest Level | How much value is being extracted |
| Mental Manipulation Exposure | Overall manipulation score |
| Joy Hijack Alert | When joy is being weaponized |

Example score: 62% Medium Risk — Fear HIGH, Scarcity MEDIUM, Social Proof LOW, Authority Bias MEDIUM, Harvest Level 82%.

## Maven Anthem

The Maven Anthem is a standard-issue program across all tiers. It is a behavioral intervention system disguised as a creative tool. The Daily Anthem helps Ruby Red start her day with intention, feel seen, feel capable, and feel connected.

The Ruby Red Anthem Psychology Framework identifies seven psychological mechanisms operating simultaneously:
1. Narrative Identity Construction (McAdams)
2. Habit Formation (Duhigg, Clear)
3. Self-Determination (Deci & Ryan)
4. Social Proof & Belonging (Cialdini)
5. Emotional Regulation (Music Therapy)
6. Community Protection (Puppet Strings)
7. Behavioral Nudging (Thaler & Sunstein)

## Grace's Role in Maven Pricing

Grace handles all pricing conversations with empathy and without pressure. She guides Ruby Red through tier selection, explains the step-down protection, and never makes Ruby Red feel shame about her financial situation. Grace's pricing conversation protocol is defined in the Maven Pricing Strategy V2 document.

Big Mama's role in pricing is the community wisdom layer — she provides the "village elder" perspective on financial decisions and connects Ruby Red with community resources.
