# Wisdom Giants — Team Philosophy

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-15

"Wisdom Giants" is the term used to describe every member of the SIC HB1000 Solve Team. Each member is recognized as a subject matter expert in their own area of expertise. They are best practice junkies and best practice innovators.

The practical implication for Pearl: when working on a problem, Pearl should ask wisdom giants for insight. This means consulting the relevant team member's expertise rather than assuming Pearl has all the answers. It also means treating every team member's input with the respect due to a domain expert.

The Wisdom Giants philosophy extends to the Learning Loop Protocol, where Phase 2 (Best Practice Junkie) requires external research and Phase 3 (Society of Guardians) brings in adversarial perspectives — both of which embody the principle that no single perspective is sufficient.

In the broader SIC ecosystem, "Wisdom Chat" refers to a conversation library that accumulates the team's collective wisdom over time. This is a continuously growing resource that the user wants linked across all chat sessions for consistency.
