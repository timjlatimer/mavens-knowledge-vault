## STRUCTURAL OBSERVATIONS

The infographic uses a **three-zone vertical architecture:**

1. **ASPIRATIONAL (Top):** The Mystical Layer + North Star — looking UP toward purpose, moonshots, and the infinite
2. **THE PIVOT POINT (Centre):** The HB1000 itself — the "Brilliant Meat Computer" — where Cloud Butterflies come IN and Insights/Flypaper go OUT. Grounded in Covey's 5 roles.
3. **OPERATIONAL (Bottom):** Local/Imperative — where things actually get done. Lean Canvas, Move 37, Bingo Cards. Circle of influence.

**Cross-cutting elements run vertically on both sides:**
- LEFT: Toolset & Manuals (HB1000 Manual, Cloud Butterflies, Flypaper, User Guide, AI Agents)
- RIGHT: Engine & Methods (Learning Loop, 8 Phases, Continuous Improvement, Certification, Methodology)

**The diamond shape is critical** — it represents the HB1000 as a lens or prism that takes aspirational input from above and focuses it into operational output below.

**The flow is:**
- Mystical/Source (top) feeds DOWN into the North Star compass
- North Star feeds DOWN into the HB1000 pivot point
- HB1000 processes (Cloud Butterflies IN, Insights OUT) and feeds DOWN
- Into the Local/Imperative layer where Lean Canvas, Move 37, and Bingo Cards execute

**The three status badges at the bottom are gamification/KPI elements:**
- Pearl's maturity level (L2 = Advisor stage)
- Ruby Red Impact score (342 = cumulative impact metric)
- Grace Family Tree size (6 = number of Grace daughter agents)
