## Moonshot Funding Model

The HB1000 supports moonshot thinking through a unique funding model:

- **50% of the cost** of chasing your moonshot is paid by the Foundation Trust (Force for Good / Social Impact Capitalism)
- Access is through the HB1000 platform
- Half of LLM research costs are covered
- Philanthropists can sign up to support someone's moonshot directly — no NGOs in between
- Supporters can agree to pay for the whole thing if they want

This model is inspired by Peter Diamandis's abundance mindset and 10X thinking, applied to Ruby Red's reality.

## Moonshot Canvas

The Moonshot Canvas is a planned feature (not yet built) within the Pearl dashboard. It will be a dedicated tab where users can:

- Explore the Diamandis/Wissner-Gross "Solve Everything" moonshot library
- Define their own moonshots using the 10X framework
- Track progress against moonshot goals
- Connect moonshots to their BHAG and North Star
- Access reference material from the "Solve Everything" paper

The Moonshot Canvas includes a token subsidy incentive for moonshot work.

## Connection to the Bingo Card

Each moonshot can be expressed as a Bingo Card with its own squares (milestones, tasks, reflections) and its own multi (AI agent) assigned to support progress. Moonshot Bingo Cards connect to the Initiative Dashboard and feed into the ticker tape metrics.
