## CLARIFICATION 1: V13 IS AN INDEPENDENT AUDIT TOOL

V13 (the Learning Loop protocol) is a **standalone evaluation tool**. It sits outside the PEARL Diamond and looks in. It does NOT get embedded into the 3D model or into V14's operating system.

- **V13** = The quality inspector that walks through the factory
- **V14** = The factory (the operating system, visualized as the PEARL Diamond)
- **3D PEARL Diamond** = The visualization of V14's architecture

When we want to improve something, we **point V13 at it**. V13 evaluates V14. V13 evaluates initiatives. V13 evaluates the 3D model. But V13 remains independent and separate.

**Rule:** Never merge V13 into the 3D Diamond. Never embed the audit tool into the thing being audited.

## CLARIFICATION 2: TWO NORTH STARS — PRIME AND LOCAL

The PEARL Diamond has a **two-tier North Star hierarchy**:

### Prime Imperative North Star (Upper Diamond)
- **Location:** Aspirational Apex → North Star Compass → THE HB1000
- **Scope:** The strategic vision for the entire SIC Solve Team
- **Focus:** Tim's 32 initiatives, the whole operating system, changing the world
- **Question it answers:** "What is the SIC Solve Team trying to achieve?"

### Local Imperative North Star (Lower Diamond)
- **Location:** Blue Seal Local → Local 4 Imperative
- **Scope:** Initiative-specific, one per initiative
- **Focus:** The specific client/persona/problem that a particular initiative serves
- **Question it answers:** "Who exactly are we solving for in THIS initiative?"

Each Local North Star must be **aligned to the Prime Imperative** but serves a different population, problem, or context.

---

## CLARIFICATION 3: RUBY RED IS A LOCAL NORTH STAR EXAMPLE

Ruby Red — the 35-45 year old household CFO making difficult decisions every day — is:

- **A Local North Star** for specific initiatives (CashCo, Maven financial tools, etc.)
- **NOT the default North Star** for everything
- **NOT the Prime Imperative North Star**
- **A sample case** — the first and most vivid persona that demonstrates the pattern
- **The proof of concept** — "if we get it right for her, we know the pattern works"

### Why this matters:
In earlier work, Ruby Red was treated as the universal default client-centric persona. As the system scales to 32 initiatives, different initiatives will have different Local North Stars:

| Initiative | Possible Local North Star |
|---|---|
| CashCo | Ruby Red (household CFO) |
| Maven Platform | Could be a different persona |
| Digger Cafe | Could be a community member |
| Seba Hub | Could be a small business owner |
| Effn Duck | Could be a different population entirely |

Ruby Red remains the **gold standard example** of how to define a Local North Star with empathy and specificity. But she is one of many, not the only one.

### The empathy principle still applies universally:
> "There for the grace of God go I"

This principle lives in the **Prime Imperative North Star** (JOY Substrate, upper diamond). It applies to ALL Local North Stars, not just Ruby Red. Every initiative, regardless of its specific Local North Star, operates with the same empathy, the same recognition that "it's expensive to be poor," and the same commitment to solving for the left out and left behind.
