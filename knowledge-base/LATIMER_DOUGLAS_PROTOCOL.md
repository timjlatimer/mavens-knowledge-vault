## What the LDP Is

The Latimer Douglas Protocol is an **autonomous, AI-driven meta-layer** for organizations. It is NOT a dashboard for humans to look at — it is an operating system that runs beneath the surface of an organization, embedding integrity into operations.

The protocol is named after Lewis Latimer (inventor, Edison's colleague) and Frederick Douglass (abolitionist, orator) — two figures who combined technical brilliance with moral purpose.

---

## The 5-Level Biomimetic Architecture

The LDP uses a biological metaphor to describe five levels of organizational capability:

| Level | Name | Function |
|-------|------|----------|
| 1 | **Memory** | Perfect recall of every commitment, decision, and interaction |
| 2 | **Perception** | Real-time awareness of organizational health and external threats |
| 3 | **Rehearsal** | Scenario planning and simulation before action |
| 4 | **Morphology** | Adaptive structure that reshapes based on conditions |
| 5 | **Immunity** | Self-healing systems that detect and respond to threats |

## PTK — Promises To Keep

PTK is the **entry point** (Trojan Horse) for the entire LDP ecosystem. It is a commitment tracking system that:

- Connects to email (read-only OAuth)
- Extracts promises and deadlines from communication
- Provides a Promise Feed dashboard
- Delivers Morning Briefings
- Tracks promises made TO you (Ghost Buster) and BY you
- Generates reliability scores

The go-to-market strategy is product-led growth via PTK. The idea is to "infect organizations with competence from inside out" — once one person uses PTK, the value spreads to everyone they interact with.

## Stage Two — Entrepreneur Insurance Guarantee

Stage Two extends the LDP into entrepreneurship support. It provides up to **$100K capital guarantee** for entrepreneurs who operate within the LDP framework. The guarantee is conditional on operating within the protocol's integrity standards.

---

## Blue Seal

The Blue Seal is a **verified integrity marker** for businesses operating within the LDP. It serves as a trust signal for consumers and partners — a visible indicator that a business has embedded integrity into its operations through the protocol.

---

## The 10% Tithe

The LDP includes a **structural commitment** of 10% of top-line revenue back to community. This is not optional or aspirational — it is built into the operating model as a non-negotiable element.

| **Economic Engine** | Entrepreneurs | Capital guarantee, integrity framework, Blue Seal |

## The Core Bet

The LDP's fundamental thesis is: **Integrity embedded into operations creates success. Success creates capital. Capital protects the next generation.**

This is the virtuous cycle the protocol is designed to create — a self-reinforcing loop where doing the right thing is also the economically optimal thing.
