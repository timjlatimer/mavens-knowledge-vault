Vasuman's 5 Rules for Enterprise AI Agents in 2026:

1. **Don't do one automation at a time** — Map ALL workflows, identify ROI, design a bespoke agent that tackles several at once. "A true agent, not an automation." Vibe-coding individual automations = maintenance nightmare.

2. **Custom-built is the only way** — "A generic tool doesn't know your exceptions, your edge cases, or why your team does step 3 before step 2. The agent has to be built on your operations."

3. **Agents get better every week** — "Human feedback, error correction, edge case handling. We've seen agents go from ~70% accuracy to 99%+ within 4 weeks." That learning curve is the difference between pilot and production.

4. **Governance from day one** — "Build auditability and human-in-the-loop controls from day one, not slapped on top after-the-fact. The best agents are built by people who literally do not trust them at all."

5. **You need to own everything** — "If your AI vendor disappears and your operations break, then you have a liability, not AI. Agents should run on your infrastructure, integrate with your stack, and work independently."
