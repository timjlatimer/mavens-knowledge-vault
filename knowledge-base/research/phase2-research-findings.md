# Phase 2 Research Findings

## Key External Best Practices Found

### 1. Bill Negotiation Services (Billshark, etc.)
- 90% success rate reported by Billshark (CNBC Select)
- Average annual savings of $300/customer
- They take a percentage of savings as fee
- **Village Opportunity**: Instead of paying a service, train a village member to be the negotiator

### 2. 211 Helpline (United Way)
- Free, confidential service available 24/7
- Connects to local utility assistance programs
- Covers all states
- **Village Opportunity**: One person calls 211, maps ALL local resources, shares with village

### 3. LIHEAP (Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program)
- Federal program, state-administered
- Helps with heating, cooling, weatherization
- Income-based eligibility
- Application windows vary by state
- **Village Opportunity**: Group application events, shared knowledge of deadlines

### 4. Community Solar Programs
- DOE reports expanding access for renters and low-income
- No rooftop needed - subscribe to a solar farm
- Savings of 10-20% on electric bills
- **Village Opportunity**: Bulk enrollment, cooperative ownership models

### 5. Community Action Agencies
- Local nonprofits that administer utility assistance
- Often have emergency funds for shutoff prevention
- Can connect to weatherization programs
- **Village Opportunity**: Build relationship as a community, not just individuals

### 6. NerdWallet Script Tactics
- Highlight loyalty and payment history
- Ask about rebates and assistance programs
- Request alternative payment plans
- Be kind but firm
- **Village Opportunity**: Refine scripts based on what actually works locally

### 7. On-Bill Financing (EESI)
- Utility companies finance energy efficiency upgrades
- Repaid through utility bill savings
- No upfront cost
- **Village Opportunity**: Bulk applications, shared contractor relationships

## Gaps in Our Guide to Address
1. **211 helpline not mentioned** - Critical resource
2. **Community solar** - Major savings opportunity not covered
3. **On-bill financing** - Free efficiency upgrades
4. **Bill negotiation services** - Option for those who can't negotiate themselves
5. **Timing matters** - Best times to call, seasonal programs
6. **Documentation checklist** - What to have ready before calling
7. **Accessibility** - Non-English speakers, hearing impaired, anxiety
