# Do Good Give Back

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-15

"Do Good Give Back" is a philosophy and potentially a program within the SIC portfolio that embodies the team's commitment to social impact. It connects directly to the "Business as a Force for Good" (BAFG) umbrella identity.

The concept appears in Gmail search results and is part of the broader ethos that business should create positive social outcomes, not just financial returns. It aligns with the "Purpose with Profit" ethics framework used as the default in the Learning Loop Protocol.

In the Learning Loop V13.0, the "Gamification Gift-Back" feature (V12.9 amendment) may be a direct expression of this philosophy — after running a learning loop on an initiative, the protocol produces a gamification layer as a "gift back" to the initiative.
