<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss International Style / Digital Brutalism Fusion
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Grid Precision**: Absolute adherence to a strict modular grid system. Everything aligns perfectly.
  2.  **Information Hierarchy**: Size and weight dictate importance. No decorative elements without function.
  3.  **High Contrast**: Stark black background, pure white text, and a single, electric accent color (Red #FF3333).
  4.  **Raw Data**: The interface feels like a high-end diagnostic tool or a cockpit display.

  **Color Philosophy**: "Mission Critical." The deep black (#050505) represents the void of the unknown. The electric red (#FF3333) represents the "alert" and "action" of the Learning Loop. White is purely for clarity. No soft greys or pastels.

  **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric Balance. A fixed sidebar for navigation/status, with a dynamic, grid-based main content area. The "Phase Tracker" is a vertical timeline that anchors the left side.

  **Signature Elements**:
  *   **Monospace Data**: All variable data (timestamps, scores, IDs) is in a high-quality monospace font (JetBrains Mono or similar).
  *   **The "Pulse"**: Active phases have a subtle, rhythmic glowing pulse animation.
  *   **Borders as Structure**: Thin, 1px borders define all content areas. No cards with drop shadows; only lines.

  **Interaction Philosophy**: "Tactile Digital." Buttons have immediate, sharp hover states (invert colors). Transitions are instant or very fast ease-out.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Entrance**: Staggered reveal of grid lines, then content snaps into place.
  *   **Live Updates**: New log entries flash briefly when added.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: Inter (Black/Heavy weight), tight tracking.
  *   **Body**: Inter (Regular).
  *   **Data**: JetBrains Mono or Roboto Mono.
</idea>

## Selected Approach: Swiss International Style / Digital Brutalism Fusion

**Reasoning**: This aligns perfectly with the user's explicit preference for "Swiss International Style" in the presentations. It reinforces the brand identity of the "Learning Loop" as a precise, rigorous protocol. The "Mission Control" vibe fits the "Proctor" and "Guardian" personas perfectly.

**Implementation Details**:
*   **Background**: #0A0A0A (Deep Black)
*   **Text**: #FFFFFF (White)
*   **Accent**: #E63946 (The "Ruby Red" aligned accent)
*   **Font**: Inter (Variable weight) + JetBrains Mono (Data)
*   **Grid**: 12-column modular grid.
*   **Borders**: 1px solid #333333.
