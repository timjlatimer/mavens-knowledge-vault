## What Cloud Butterflies Are

Cloud Butterflies are the raw, unfiltered thoughts, ideas, and insights that emerge from stream-of-consciousness brainstorming. They are the "unconscious insights" that flutter through the mind — often during early morning hours, in the shower, or while driving. The Cloud Butterfly Protocol is the system for capturing, processing, and acting on these insights.

The protocol distinguishes between two types of captured content:

| Type | Description | Processing |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Cloud Butterflies** | Unconscious insights, creative ideas, strategic thoughts | Captured → Deduplicated → Organized → Routed to appropriate initiative |
| **PTK (Promises To Keep)** | Conscious commitments — things promised TO you or BY you | Captured → Tracked → Monitored → Escalated if overdue |

---

## The Protocol Architecture (v4.0)

Version 4.0 is a **drop-in system** designed to work with any AI engine. It provides:

1. **Capture Layer** — Always-on input for voice, text, or paste. Pearl ingests the raw stream.
2. **Processing Layer** — Deduplication, organization, extraction of actionable items.
3. **Grace Items** — Action items routed to the appropriate Grace agent for execution.
4. **Ghost Buster** — Tracks promises made BY others TO you. Monitors for follow-through.
5. **Persistent State** — State tracking across sessions. Nothing is lost.

---

## Key Concepts

**Flypaper** is the always-on capture interface within the HB1000. It is the "sticky surface" where cloud butterflies land. Users can paste, dictate, or record butterflies at any time. Pearl processes them in the background.

**Grace Items** are the actionable outputs from cloud butterfly processing. Each Grace Item includes the action to take, the contact involved, the priority level, pre-drafted communications, and deadline tracking. Grace Items can be approved, snoozed, escalated, or batch-processed.

**Ghost Buster** is the reverse-PTK system. While PTK tracks promises YOU made, Ghost Buster tracks promises OTHERS made to you. It monitors for follow-through and escalates when commitments are not met.

**Auto-Escalation** occurs when Grace Items age beyond their expected resolution window. Items that were LOW or MEDIUM priority get automatically upgraded based on age and context.

## The Daily Report

The Cloud Butterfly Report is generated daily and includes:

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Summary Cards | YTD butterfly count, today's count, pending Grace items, escalated items |
| Deadline Items | Items with hard deadlines, sorted by urgency |
| Escalated Items | Previously low-priority items that have aged into higher priority |
| Approved Awaiting Send | Items approved but not yet dispatched |
| Draft Communications | Pre-written emails ready for review and send |
| Weekly Digest | Aggregated view of the week's activity |
