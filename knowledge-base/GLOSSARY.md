# Master Glossary — All Terms, Acronyms, and Concepts

**Source:** Compiled from all documents in Pearl's Brain

---

## A

**Atomic Habits** — James Clear's framework for identity-based habit change. The Bingo Card is the HB1000's implementation of Atomic Habits — small, visible, completable actions that vote for your identity.

---

## B

**Bee Chart** — The assessment tool for measuring where an organization sits on the SIC Matrix. Produces a score on both the Economic Engine and Purpose Alignment axes.

**BHAG** — Big Hairy Audacious Goal (Jim Collins). Applied to personal life in the HB1000 as the user's moonshot / North Star.

**Big Mama's Genius Village** — The community brand within Maven. "Our Data. Our Power. Our Destiny." Fueled by JOY. Provides protection and empowerment services to Ruby Red.

**Bingo Card** — The universal input/output format for the entire HB1000 system. Each card is a context (North Star, daily tasks, etc.) containing squares (tasks, questions, actions). See BINGO_CARD_PROTOCOL.md.

**Blue Seal** — Verified integrity marker for businesses operating within the LDP. A trust signal for consumers and partners.

**Bullshit Detector** — A Big Mama Village service that identifies manipulation in communications and offers directed at Ruby Red.

---

## C

**Cloud Butterflies** — Raw, unfiltered thoughts and insights captured through stream-of-consciousness brainstorming. The unconscious insights that flutter through the mind. See CLOUD_BUTTERFLY_PROTOCOL.md.

**Creep Watch** — A Big Mama Village safety monitoring service for vulnerable community members.

**Crystallized Intelligence** — Lived experience never written down. The wisdom of Wisdom Giants. Grows throughout life (unlike Fluid Intelligence which peaks in the 20s).

**CVO** — Chief Visionary Officer. The user's role within the SIC HB1000 Solve Team.

---

## D

**Destiny Discovered** — Tim Latimer's 30+ year study of 1,600+ biographies. Core finding: "What makes the great, great is a sense of destiny." Three requirements: Purpose, Opportunity to Contribute, Developing Legacy.

**Dual-Engine Protocol** — The Solve Team's operating system (v5.0). Engine A: Learning Loop (self-correcting iteration). Engine B: Web-Core Canvas (visual generation).

---

## E

**Economic Floor** — One of the LDP's three pillars. Serves the working poor (Ruby Red) through protection, access, and dignity.

**Economic Engine** — One of the LDP's three pillars. Serves entrepreneurs through capital guarantee, integrity framework, and Blue Seal.

---

## F

**Flypaper** — The always-on capture interface within the HB1000. The "sticky surface" where cloud butterflies land.

**Force for Good** — The philanthropic arm of the SIC ecosystem. Provides the 50% moonshot funding match.

**Fossilized Intelligence** — AI and documented knowledge. Searchable, scalable, instant. One half of the Wisdom Works equation.

---

## G

**Ghost Buster** — The reverse-PTK system. Tracks promises OTHERS made to you and monitors for follow-through.

**Grace** — Pearl's daughter. The project-level AI agent that does specific work. Each project gets its own Grace. See GRACE_IDENTITY.md.

**Grace Godmother** — The executive layer that manages all Project Graces. Reports to Pearl.

**Grace Item** — An actionable output from cloud butterfly processing. Includes action, contact, priority, pre-drafted communications, and deadline tracking.

---

## H

**HB** — Human Brilliance. A component of the LDEI Score measuring quality of human contribution.

**HB1000** — The Operating System for Being Human. A culturally universal, AI-powered platform for operating life with intention, wisdom, and support. See HB1000_FRAMEWORK.md.

**HITL** — Human In The Loop. The approval mechanism that ensures Pearl never acts without human authorization on sensitive matters.

**Honeybee Zone** — The sweet spot on the SIC Matrix (7-8 range) where profit and purpose are force multipliers.

---

## I

**Ikigai** — Japanese concept meaning "reason for being." Intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

**Initiative Dashboard** — The primary home screen for Pearl. A visual grid of all active initiatives as Bingo Cards.

---

## J

**Jolene Protocol** — Emmy-winning journalist's narrative framework: Opening Hook, Physical Object, Quote Discipline, The Kicker, Restraint. The storytelling protocol for the entire ecosystem.

**JOY** — The fuel for Big Mama's Genius Village. JOY Nudges are positive behavioral interventions disguised as enjoyable interactions.

**JTBD** — Job To Be Done. Ruby Red's JTBD: feel seen, feel less alone, feel capable, feel connected, start the day with intention.

---

## L

**LDEI Score** — LDP Engagement Index. Combines PTK Score + Human Brilliance (HB) + Cloud Butterflies.

**LDP** — Latimer Douglas Protocol. An autonomous, AI-driven meta-layer for organizations with a 5-level biomimetic architecture. See LATIMER_DOUGLAS_PROTOCOL.md.

**Learning Loop** — The self-correcting iteration protocol (Engine A of the Dual-Engine Protocol). Currently at v12.9.1 / v13.0 ("The Genie Release").

---

## M

**Maven** — The community platform that serves Ruby Red directly. The implementation layer where HB1000 philosophy meets real people. See MAVEN_PROJECT.md.

**Moonshot** — A 10X goal (Diamandis). The HB1000 supports 15 moonshots across 4 categories. 50% funding from Foundation Trust.

**Move 37** — The creative breakthrough tool. Named after AlphaGo's famous unexpected move. A space for Pearl and the user to apply breakthrough thinking to any challenge.

**Multi** — An AI agent attached to a Bingo Card. An imported Grace that takes care of the work within that card's context.

---

## N

**Need Milk Money (NMM)** — Maven's micro-lending program. Allotments of $25, $50, or $100 with a $2.50 admin fee. No interest, no penalties, no collections. Replaced the V1 Community Chest model.

**Neumorphic Futurism** — Grace's design language. Soft, approachable UI with depth.

**North Star** — The user's life purpose and direction. Configured during Pearl's Identity Setup and referenced throughout the HB1000.

---

## P

**Pearl** — The Mother of All Graces. The Universal Matriarchal AI Framework. An autonomous, persistent, learning digital guardian. See PEARL_IDENTITY.md.

**Pearl's Brain** — The persistent knowledge base maintained by Pearl. Includes master file catalog, knowledge base entries, cloud butterfly archive, PTK database, and ecosystem map.

**PERMA** — Martin Seligman's model: Positive emotions, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning, Accomplishment. The HB1000 should track all five.

**PTK** — Promises To Keep. A commitment tracking system. Tracks promises made TO you and BY you. The entry point (Trojan Horse) for the LDP ecosystem.

**Puppet Strings Report** — Measures manipulation exposure: Fear, Scarcity, Social Proof, Authority Bias, Harvest Level, Mental Manipulation Exposure, Joy Hijack Alert.

---

## R

**Ruby Red** — The target user persona. The CFO of the household. 35-45 year old mother of two trying to make finances stretch to next payday. Working poor, unbanked/underbanked. See WHO_WE_ARE.md.

---

## S

**Schwartz Values** — 10 universal human values validated across 80+ countries. Organized in a circle with two fundamental tensions: Openness vs. Conservation, Self-Enhancement vs. Self-Transcendence.

**SIC** — Social Impact Capitalism. The belief that profit and purpose are force multipliers when combined at the right ratio.

**SIC Matrix** — Two-axis assessment: Economic Engine Strength vs. Purpose Alignment. Four zones: Deadwood, Martyr, Honeybee (sweet spot), SOB.

**Step-Down** — Maven's payment protection mechanism. When Ruby Red can't pay, Maven opens a smaller door instead of locking it. Step-down is a feature, not a failure.

---

## T

**Three Gangsters** — The three systems that exploit Ruby Red: Political (learned helplessness), Bureaucratic (cognitive punishment), Greedy Capitalist (extraction).

**Ticker Tape** — Running status bar at the bottom of the Pearl dashboard. Up to two tapes with customizable feeds.

**10% Tithe** — Structural commitment of 10% of top-line revenue back to community. Non-negotiable.

**Toilet Paper Sam** — Maven community mascot moment. Sam surrounded by toilet paper rolls. Represents the proof concept: "If we can guarantee toilet paper, we can guarantee anything."

---

## U

**Ubuntu** — African philosophy: "I am because we are." Personhood defined through community. Philosophical foundation for the community/support model.

---

## W

**Wisdom Giants** — Subject matter experts with deep lived experience. Each team member is a wisdom giant. In the broader system, Wisdom Giants are community contributors who share crystallized intelligence.

**Wisdom Works** — Standalone global product. A query strategy for the AI Age combining Fossilized Intelligence and Crystallized Intelligence. See WISDOM_GIANTS.md.
