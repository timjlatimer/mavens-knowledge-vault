## Site Overview

The **Maven Service Tier Voting Dashboard** is a collaborative decision-making tool used by the SIC HB1000 Solve Team to vote on which services should be included in each pricing tier of the **Big Mama / Maven** platform. The platform is designed to serve **Ruby Red** — the team's client-centric persona representing the working poor, unbanked, and underbanked community member (typically a 35–45 year old mother managing household finances under significant financial stress).

The voting system allows five team members — **Fadell, Sam, Trina, Michelle, and Tim** — to cast votes of **Certain / Maybe / No** for each service across five pricing tiers.
