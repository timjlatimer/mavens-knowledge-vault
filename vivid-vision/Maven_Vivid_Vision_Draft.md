## Top 3 Recommendations

1. **"A village of support, a lifetime of grace."**
   This statement beautifully weaves the concept of Big Mama's Village with the persistent, supportive presence of Grace. It offers immediate comfort (support) while promising a long-term, dignified future (lifetime of grace).
2. **"Ending the poverty penalty, one family forward."**
   Directly addressing the "expensive to be poor" reality, this is a rallying cry. It shows Ruby that Maven is actively fighting the systemic issues she faces, offering a tangible path forward.
3. **"Empowering Ruby to plant tomorrow's shade trees."**
   This speaks directly to the 30-year vision. It acknowledges Ruby's current efforts and elevates them to a legacy-building level, assuring her that her hard work today will protect her children tomorrow.

## Destiny Statement

Maven is building a future where the systemic penalty of poverty is eradicated, replaced by a robust, interconnected village that empowers the working poor to thrive. We are moving toward a reality where every family, supported by persistent AI companionship and collective bargaining power, has the dignity, resources, and community necessary to plant shade trees for generations to come. In this destiny, survival is no longer the daily goal; generational prosperity is the expectation.

## 5 Core Values

1. **Radical Empathy:** We operate with the profound understanding that "there but for the grace of God go I," ensuring every solution is designed with deep compassion for Ruby's daily reality.
2. **Collective Power:** We believe in the strength of the Union Boss and the village; together, we negotiate better realities and build systems that work for the many, not just the few.
3. **Generational Foresight:** We are committed to the 30-year vision, making decisions today that will grow amazing children and plant trees whose shade we may never sit under.
4. **Local Resilience:** Through the Blue Seal, we prioritize keeping resources within the community, fostering local economic strength and mutual support.
5. **Dignified Companionship:** Like Grace, we provide persistent, non-judgmental, and empowering support, recognizing that everyone deserves an advocate in their corner.
