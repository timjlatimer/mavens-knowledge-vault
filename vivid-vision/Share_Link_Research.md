## Part 1: Freedom Cannabis Seed-to-Sale Analysis

**Source:** [https://manus.im/share/aqCK4OUTZPkYfwWFYzygRR](https://manus.im/share/aqCK4OUTZPkYfwWFYzygRR)

### Overview
This session involved the creation and subsequent rollback of an interactive web application for the "Freedom Cannabis Seed-to-Sale Interactive Deck." The team ultimately decided to maintain the 13-slide PowerPoint presentation as the authoritative baseline.

### Strategic Frameworks & Vision Language
The presentation outlines a comprehensive 100-step process for the cannabis sector, which serves as a powerful metaphor for complex operational architectures. Key strategic elements extracted include:

*   **The Mission:** Explicitly identified as a **"Ruby Red Focus."** This confirms that even in sector-specific workflows like cannabis, the ultimate beneficiary and focal point remains the core client archetype.
*   **Actor Zones:** The framework categorizes operational environments into distinct zones, notably identifying:
    *   **Chaos Cowboys:** Representing the unpredictable, execution-focused environments (growers/retail).
    *   **Gangster Bureaucrats:** Representing the regulatory and compliance environments. This directly maps to the "three gangster worlds" concept central to the team's problem-solving approach.
*   **The North Star:** Step 101 is defined as **"Surprise & Delight."** This indicates that the ultimate goal of any process is not merely completion, but exceeding expectations and providing a moment of joy or relief for the client.
*   **Feedback Mechanism:** The inclusion of a **"Closing The Loop - Market Intelligence Feedback"** step emphasizes the importance of continuous learning and adaptation based on real-world outcomes.

### Visual Design Patterns for Maven Admin Dashboard
The session highlighted several visual and functional patterns that should be considered for the Maven admin dashboard:

1.  **Swiss International Style:** The presentation utilized a clean, structured, and highly legible design language. The dashboard should adopt this aesthetic to minimize cognitive load.
2.  **Interactive 100-Step Visualization:** The ability to visualize a complex, multi-stage process (like a user journey or financial recovery plan) with clear ownership zones.
3.  **Comment Threading & Zonal Notifications:** The proposed feature to allow team members to comment on specific steps and notify stakeholders based on their "ownership zones" (e.g., Steps 26-75 vs. 76-100) is highly relevant for case management in the dashboard.

### Additions to Maven Innovation Index
*   **Innovation:** **The 100-Step Journey Map with Actor Zones**
*   **Description:** A visualization framework that maps complex processes while explicitly identifying the "Actor Zones" (e.g., Chaos Cowboys, Gangster Bureaucrats) involved at each stage, clarifying where Ruby Red faces the most friction.
*   **Impact:** Helps the Solve Team pinpoint exactly where intervention or advocacy is needed.
