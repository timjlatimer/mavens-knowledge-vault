## The Mission
"It's expensive to be poor. We think that's a crime in society, and we're trying to change it." 
Big Mama is the community connection leg of the Three-Legged Stool — the platform that reconnects Ruby to her local neighborhood, mutual aid networks, and village resources. We recognize that our superpower with Ruby Red is empathy, and we practice it with a sense of "there for the grace of God go I".
