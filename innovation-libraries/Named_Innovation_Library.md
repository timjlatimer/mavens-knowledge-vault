## The Mission

> *"It's expensive to be poor. We think that's a crime in society, and we're trying to change it."*

Big Mama is the community connection leg of the Three-Legged Stool — the platform that reconnects Ruby to her local neighborhood, mutual aid networks, and village resources. Every name in this library was written as if Ruby is talking to her neighbor over the fence. Not a corporate brochure. Not a pitch deck. Ruby's voice.

**The Ruby Test:** Would a 35–45 year old working-poor mom smile, feel seen, or feel hopeful hearing this name? If yes — it passes.

## Naming Principles Reference

All names in this library were created under the following constraints:

- **Ruby Red's voice:** authentic, transparent, vulnerable, warm, sometimes sassy
- **The Fence Test:** names should feel like Ruby talking to her neighbor over the fence — not a corporate brochure
- **Playful when appropriate:** "The NSF Ninja", "Milk Money", "The Corner"
- **Urgent when necessary:** "The Flare", "The Watch", "The Safety Net"
- **Community-rooted:** "The Village Petition", "The Neighbor Knock", "Eyes on the Block"
- **The Ruby Test:** would a 35–45 year old working-poor mom smile, feel seen, or feel hopeful hearing this name?
- **8 words or less** for every name
- **15 words or less** for every tagline
